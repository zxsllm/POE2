Atlas Passive

"Atlas Passive Points" are rewarded by completing a specified quest. There are currently 7 Atlas Passive regions.

"Core" passive tree: (40 points)
  • Complete a Nexus of Corruption to earn points in this tree.
  • Additional points are earned for completing Nexus' of Corruption at higher Waystone Tiers.
  • 10 Additional points are also earned for completing different Unique Maps.
  • You must be the Map Owner to earn points.
"Map Boss" passive tree: (10 points)
  • 2: Slaying a Map Boss in an area level 79+ Map that has at least 6 Modifiers.
  • 4: Slaying a Map Boss in an area level 80+ Map that has at least 6 Modifiers.
  • 6: Slaying a Anomaly Map Boss in an area level 79+ Map that has at least 6 Modifiers.
  • 8: Slaying a Citadel Map Boss in an area level 79+ Map that has at least 6 Modifiers.
  • 10: Completing the Pinnacle of Flame Quest.

Use Vaal Orb on T15 may become T16

"Breach" passive tree: (8 points)
  • First time complete grants 2 points
  • Put 50/100/150 Breach Splinter into Realm Gate and defeat grants 4/6/8 points
"Ritual" passive tree: (8 points)
  • First time complete grants 2 points
  • Put 50/100/150 Petition Splinter into Realm Gate and defeat grants 4/6/8 points
"Delirium" passive tree: (8 points)
  • First time complete grants 2 points
  • Put 50/100/150 Simulacrum Splinter into Realm Gate and defeat grants 4/6/8 points
"Expedition" passive tree: (8 points)
  • First time complete grants 2 points
  • Put 50/100/150 Runic Splinter into Realm Gate and defeat grants 4/6/8 points
